## Recommended use in backtests

1. Use direct bid/ask boundary fills as the conservative baseline for marketable orders.

2. Use latency quote fills, not spread multipliers, for latency stress.

3. For passive entry, use estimated fill probability and markout by bucket; unfilled orders should not magically become midpoint fills.

4. Reject any trade whose expected reversion is below predicted all-in cost plus an adverse-selection buffer.
