## Interpretation

A positive validation portfolio is not sufficient.  Monetization requires the same allocation to remain positive under the untouched test window and under realistic execution stress.  If the selected row is diagnostic_only or no_trade, the correct conclusion is that prediction exists in pockets, but monetization remains unproven.
